Mango Sticky Rice

Description

Glutinous rice (Oryza sativa var. glutinosa; also called sticky rice, sweet rice or waxy rice) is a type of rice grown mainly in Southeast Asia and the northeastern regions of South Asia, which has opaque grains and very low amylose content and is especially sticky when cooked.

Ingredients

  • Glutinous White Rice
  • Coconut Milk
  • Sugar
  • Tapioca Starch
  • Mango
  • Toasted Sesame Seed
